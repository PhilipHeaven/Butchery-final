Who We Are

Proudly Serving Auckland

Three Kings Butchery was founded with a simple mission: to provide Auckland's halal community with access to the highest quality, ethically slaughtered meat at fair prices. Located in the heart of Three Kings, we serve families, restaurants, and food businesses who demand the best.

Our butchers are experienced professionals who take pride in every cut. We source our meat from trusted New Zealand farms.

Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Three Kings Butchery.

How does click and collect work? ▼
Simply browse our products online, add items to your cart, and complete your purchase securely using Stripe. You'll receive an order confirmation email with your order number. Bring that order number to our store and our team will have your order ready and packaged for you.
Do you offer delivery? ▼
At this stage we offer click-and-collect only. All orders must be collected from our store in Three Kings, Auckland. We do not currently offer courier or home delivery services.
Is all your meat halal? ▼
Yes — every single product we sell is halal. Our certificate is available to view in-store at any time.
Can I see your certificate? ▼
Our certificate is displayed in-store and available on request. Please contact us if you need a copy for your records.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay — all processed securely through Stripe. Payment is taken at the time of placing your online order.
How long will it take for my order to be ready? ▼
Orders placed before 2pm are generally ready for same-day collection. Orders placed after 2pm will be ready the following business day. We'll notify you by email when your order is ready. If you need your order urgently, please give us a call.
Can I request custom cuts? ▼
Yes! While our website lists our standard range, our butchers are happy to discuss custom cuts. Please call or visit us in-store to talk through your requirements. Custom cut orders are not available online at this stage.
Do you cater for restaurants and food businesses? ▼
Yes, we supply a number of restaurants and food businesses in the Auckland area. If you're interested in wholesale or bulk orders, please get in touch via the Contact page or call us directly to discuss your needs.
What if a product is out of stock? ▼
Products marked as out of stock on the website are temporarily unavailable. Check back regularly as we update our stock daily. You can also give us a call to ask when a specific product will be back in stock.
Can I change or cancel my order? ▼
If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible by phone. We'll do our best to accommodate changes, but please note that once an order has been prepared we may not be able to cancel it.
