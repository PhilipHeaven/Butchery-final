About Three Kings
Butchery

A family-owned halal butchery serving Auckland's community with ethically sourced meat and honest service — from our counter to your kitchen.

Who We Are

Proudly Serving Auckland

Three Kings Butchery was founded with a simple mission: to provide Auckland's halal community with access to the highest quality, ethically slaughtered meat at fair prices. Located in the heart of Three Kings, we serve families, restaurants, and food businesses who demand the best.

Our butchers are experienced professionals who take pride in every cut. We source our meat from trusted New Zealand farms and ensure every product meets the highest standards of quality and care.

Help Centre

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, collection, and our products.

How does click and collect work? ▼
Simply browse our products online and place your order. We are click and collect only — there is no delivery. Once your order is placed, bring your order confirmation to our store at Three Kings and our team will have everything ready and packaged for you. Payment is made in-store on collection — we do not take payment online.
Do you offer delivery? ▼
At this stage we offer click-and-collect only. All orders must be collected from our store in Three Kings, Auckland. We do not currently offer courier or home delivery.
Is all your meat halal? ▼
Yes — every single product we sell is halal. Our certificate is available to view in-store at any time.
Can I see your certificate? ▼
Our certificate is displayed in-store and available on request. Please contact us if you need a copy for your records.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
Payment is made in-store on collection. We do not take any payment online. We accept cash, eftpos, and major credit cards in-store.
How long will it take for my order to be ready? ▼
Orders placed before 2pm are generally ready for same-day collection. Orders placed after 2pm will be ready the following business day. If you need your order urgently, please give us a call.
Can I request custom cuts? ▼
Yes! Our butchers are happy to discuss custom cuts. Please call or visit us in-store to talk through your requirements. Custom cut orders are not currently available online.
Do you cater for restaurants and food businesses? ▼
Yes, we supply a number of restaurants and food businesses in Auckland. If you're interested in wholesale or bulk orders, please get in touch via the Contact page or call us directly.
What if a product is out of stock? ▼
Products marked as out of stock are temporarily unavailable. Check back regularly as we update stock daily. You can also call us to ask when a specific product will be back.
Can I change or cancel my order? ▼
If you need to change or cancel, please contact us by phone as soon as possible. Once an order has been prepared we may not be able to cancel it.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Call us, send a message, or visit us in-store.
